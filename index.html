What is Layer 2? When is the launch?

Layer 2 is a network built on top of the Ethereum blockchain, providing ultra-fast and low-cost transactions. Thanks to its unique bullish idea, Moo.Army is becoming a global leader in exclusive technologies, opening new horizons for its users on its own blockchain!
• Increased Speed: Transactions on Layer 2 solutions are processed much faster than on the L1 network. This allows investors to instantly participate in staking, trading, and interacting with the Moo.Army ecosystem at maximum speed. • Lower Fees: By using Layer 2, the load on the main Ethereum network is reduced, significantly lowering gas fees on the L2. This enables more funds to go toward potential profits rather than covering transaction costs. MOO.ARMY L2 BLOCKCHAIN and the entire ecosystem will launch right after the presale ends!
